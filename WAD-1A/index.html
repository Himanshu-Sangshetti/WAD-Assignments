Registration Eligibility

To participate in AL CONVOCRAFT EVENT and PLACEMENT SERIES, you must meet the following criteria:

  • 18 years and above.
  • Should be pursuing a technical degree and have programming knowledge,atleast on one particular coding language(C/C++/c#/Python/Java/HTML etc.)
  • Individuals or a team of 3-6 members can participate.
  • Every topic/project must have an AI element or platform to showcase(Mandatory)
  • Registration charges for an individual is Rs.100

F.A.Q.s

  • What is Nestria Hackathon all about?

    Nestria Hackathon is a month-long online event where registered teams work on AI projects. The event includes guidance, weekly checkups, and offers placement opportunities for winning teams.

  • How can I register for the hackathon?

    To register, visit the "Register" section on our website and fill out the team registration form. Make sure to provide accurate information and meet the eligibility criteria.

  • What are the eligibility criteria for participation?

    Participants must be 18 years and above, pursuing a technical degree, and have programming knowledge in at least one coding language. Teams can have a maximum of 6 members, and each project must involve an AI element or platform.

  • Is there a registration fee?

    Yes, there is a registration fee of Rs.100 per individual. The fee helps support the event and provides participants with various perks and resources.

  • What are the perks of participating in Nestria Hackathon?

    Participants can access exclusive workshops, guidance sessions, and network with industry experts. Outstanding projects have the chance for potential placement offers, and the winning team receives a prize money of Rs.5000.

  • How is the final project evaluated?

    The final projects will be evaluated by a panel of judges. The evaluation criteria include innovation, technical proficiency, and the successful integration of AI technologies into the projects.
